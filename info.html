"SWEAT THE SMALL STUFF"

I am a visual designer from New England. I currently reside in Maine but am frequently traveling throughout the region. I love to work with small and local business to help them create no-nonsense identities that present them in a positive-light to their target audience. I specialize in logo design, branding, and web design/development.

I grew up in a blue-collar working family and I like to bring that same attitude towards my design work. I love to work with my hands and get dirty with the details. I'll draw things out, smudge em' up, tear em' up, then restart all over again. Rinse and Repeat.

Design should be timeless. Design should simplify, unite, and inform.


Let's race.

My Design Principles: While still sweating the small stuff, it is important to keep a few other little things in mind.

01

Stay curious

Never stop looking for new ideas and new resolutions to old problems.

02

Be respectful

Unite. Uplift. Dignify. Inspire. Mind your P's and Q's while thinking differently.

03

Do good work

Create timeless designs that make a positive difference that my client and I can be proud of for years to come.
